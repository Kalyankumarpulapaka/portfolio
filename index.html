My Story

I am an experienced SW/App/Cloud Tech Support Analyst with a proven track record at Accenture Solutions Pvt Ltd. I specialize in managing and maintaining virtual and physical infrastructure, including VMware vSphere environments (versions 6.5, 6.7, and 7.0) across 10+ vCenters. My expertise extends to providing L1 and L2 support for Windows servers (2012 R2, 2016, 2019) across multiple global locations, ensuring high availability and performance. I am skilled in troubleshooting issues related to server performance, folder access permissions, and RDP connections, while also handling critical incident management for business-critical applications.

In my current role, I actively monitor server performance (CPU, RAM, and HDD utilization), perform root cause analysis, manage server hardware remotely, and collaborate with top hardware vendors like DELL, Fujitsu, and Park Pace Technologies. I have extensive experience in managing change processes, from planning and risk analysis to implementation and reporting. Additionally, I have been responsible for performing server firmware updates, documenting hardware configurations, and supporting infrastructure projects through collaboration with IT and network teams.

Previously, I worked as an Infra Managed Service Associate at Accenture, where I gained experience in server hardware management, monitoring, troubleshooting, and incident resolution. My proactive approach in handling Sev-1 and Sev-2 incidents, coordinating with engineering teams, and ensuring minimal downtime has significantly contributed to maintaining operational efficiency.
